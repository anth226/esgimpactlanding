About Us

Democratizing ESG for the Modern Business

We believe that it is much easier and more impactful for a company to integrate ESG from the beginning. This will help lead the incoming generational shift of unicorns and Fortune 500 companies to prioritize more diverse and inclusive environments, employee health and safety, and environmental protection. As we move forward in this climate, every deal will be scrutinized on ESG parameters. Private equity, venture capital, and all companies will need to embed ESG across all transactions, not only those ostensibly geared to those ends.

Our mission at ESG Impact ("ESGi") is to democratize ESG for the modern business. Often, companies outsource their conscience to consultants because of the difficulties in identifying ethical business practices material to their operations. We have built an accessible resource that allows companies and funds to build their foundation from the ground up, all while keeping control of their internal operations. At ESGi, our goal is to help our clients find the prudent balance between impact and profit .
