About Us

Democratizing ESG for the Modern Business

We believe that it is much easier and more impactful for a company to integrate ESG from the beginning. This will help lead the incoming generational shift of unicorns and Fortune 500 companies that will prioritize more diverse and inclusive environments, employee health and safety, and environmental protection. As we move forward in this climate, every deal will be scrutinized on ESG parameters.Private equity, venture capital, and all comapnies will need to embed ESG across all transactions, not only those obstensibly geared to those ends.

Our mission at ESG Impact ("ESGi") is to democratize ESG for the modern business. Ethical practices and frameworks should not be a costly endeavor. Our goal is to help our clients find the prudent balance between impact and profit . Not all companies need to prioritize ESG, but all need to abide by ethical standards.
